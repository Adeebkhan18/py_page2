About Us

An initiative by Ofbeatt Psychology (MSME Registered, ISO certified, StartupIndia Recognised & WFMH Member)

Art Therapy Journaling is a gentle, creative, and deeply personal way to heal and understand yourself. It combines the power of visual art and reflective writing backed by research in psychology, neuroscience and mental health to help you express emotions, process experiences, and reconnect with your inner world even when words fall short so that you can become the best version of yourself. .

By engaging in Art Therapy Journaling, you can:

  • Reduce stress and anxiety through mindful creative expression.
  • Gain self-awareness and understand your emotions more deeply.
  • Build self-compassion and a stronger sense of identity
  • Release emotional blockages and feel lighter and calmer
  • Boost creativity and positivity in your daily life

Enrolling in the 6-Day Art Therapy Journaling Course will help you slow down, reflect, and heal - one page, one color, and one emotion at a time. It’s not just art therapy journaling. It’s a journey back to you!

Know your Mentor

Ms. Tanishka Safri is the founder of 'Ofbeatt Psychology' an organisation committed to providing Mental Health services to the masses at affordable prices. She is a Ph.D. in Psychology and is a certified Art Therapist, Career Counselor and Hypnotherapist. She has helped several individuals by means of counseling and psychotherapy. Through her Youtube channel ‘Ofbeatt Psychology’ she is creating awareness about the importance of Mental Health and careers in the field of Psychology. She specializes in Loneliness counseling and is actively working to help people overcome problems of loneliness. Ms. Tanishka is also an expert in creating and administering Psychometric Tests, with her tests being sold on Amazon India. During her stay in Lucknow, she has been actively involved with different NGO’s for carrying out social service, doing psychometric profiling for her clients and much more. Her research papers have been published in Journals of national and international repute. Through her expertise in Art Therapy she has empowered hundreds of individuals to heal themselves and reconnect with their higher self using the power of Art Therapy.
